Property Description

Welcome to your new home in the heart of Mission Valley! This recently renovated 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom condo offers modern comfort and convenience, making it the perfect retreat for anyone looking to enjoy the vibrant lifestyle of San Diego.

Step inside and be greeted by a spacious, fully furnished living space that features new luxury vinyl plank flooring throughout. The large living room opens up to a cozy patio, just 15 steps away from one of the complex's sparkling pools - perfect for enjoying the California sunshine. The condo is equipped with central A/C and heating, ensuring you stay comfortable year-round.

This property includes one covered reserved parking spot, along with two additional parking passes, making it easy for you and your guests. The complex itself boasts an array of amenities, including three pools, two hot tubs, a gym, a sauna, and courts for pickleball, tennis, and basketball. Outdoor BBQs, a dog run, and onsite laundry facilities add to the convenience and appeal of this community.

Location is everything, and this condo delivers. You'll be less than a mile from Snapdragon Stadium, and just minutes from some of San Diego's most popular neighborhoods, including North Park, Hillcrest, and Downtown. For beach lovers, Pacific Beach and Ocean Beach are just a 15-minute drive away.

Whether you're looking to relax by the pool, stay active with the complex's many amenities, or explore the surrounding areas, this Mission Valley condo offers it all. Don't miss your chance to make this your new home!
